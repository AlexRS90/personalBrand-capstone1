Why RASA Running? Since 2017 I started to run, just as a fun activity that I did with my family, after a few weekends running on Sunday, I noticed all the enjoyable time that I spend while running, so, here is when the storey begins. I enrolled in my first race, and after in the second and continue until I saw that I want more, more challenges for me. I started the race to earn the Abbot Marathon Major Medal, only people who run the six majors can earn it. I'm writing this page expecting to inspire someone to start to run.
